What we offer

Everything a logistics company
needs, out of the box

From job creation to proof of delivery, real-time driver tracking to automated invoicing — all in one connected, multi-tenant platform.

📦
Smart Job Management

Create, assign, and track deliveries through a complete lifecycle — draft → assigned → in transit → delivered — with timestamps and an immutable audit log at every step.

🗺️
Live Driver Tracking

Drivers update GPS every 30 seconds via the Flutter app. Customers and admins watch deliveries move in real time on Google Maps via Firestore streams — zero polling required.

🧾
Automated Invoicing

When a job is marked delivered, a Cloud Function instantly generates a PDF invoice, uploads to Firebase Storage, and emails it to the customer — no manual work needed.

📸
Proof of Delivery

Drivers capture photos, collect digital signatures, and record GPS coordinates at the delivery point. All evidence is stored securely in Firebase Storage, namespaced by tenant.

🏢
Multi-Tenant Isolation

Every document carries a companyId enforced by Firestore Security Rules and JWT claims. Company A can never see a byte of Company B's data.

📈
Analytics & Reporting

Pro and Enterprise customers unlock revenue charts, driver performance reports, success rates, and monthly KPI dashboards — computed server-side and never exposing raw data to the client.

Angular admin dashboard for full operational control

Company administrators get a complete command centre — manage drivers, create and assign jobs, handle customer accounts, view analytics, and manage billing — all scoped strictly to their own company.

  • Real-time KPI dashboard — jobs, revenue, driver count, success rate
  • Job board with status filters, search, and one-click driver assignment
  • Live fleet map showing every driver's GPS location
  • Customer CRM with credit limits and payment terms
  • Invoice management with PDF generation and email delivery
  • User management — invite admins, dispatchers, and customers
  • Plan-gated analytics module (Pro & Enterprise only)

  • GPS coordinates auto-captured with each status change
